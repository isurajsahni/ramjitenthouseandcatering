Our Story

Leading Tent House in Ludhiana & Punjab

Founded in 2008, Ram Ji Tent House and Catering has been serving Punjab with excellence in event management, tent rental, catering, and decoration services. What started as a small tent rental business in Ludhiana has now grown into one of Punjab's most trusted names in the event industry.

Over the past 15+ years, we have successfully organized more than 5000 events including weddings, corporate functions, religious ceremonies, and social gatherings across Punjab. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and attention to detail has made us the preferred choice for event services in Ludhiana, Jalandhar, Amritsar, and beyond.

Today, Ram Ji Tent House and Catering stands as a comprehensive event solutions provider with a team of experienced professionals, premium quality materials, and a track record of delivering memorable events.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional event services that exceed customer expectations. We aim to make every celebration memorable through our dedication, quality materials, professional execution, and personalized attention to each event. Our mission is to be Punjab's most reliable and customer-focused event solutions provider.

Our Vision

To become North India's leading event management company known for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We envision expanding our services while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that has made us successful. Our goal is to set new standards in the tent house and event management industry.
